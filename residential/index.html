About the Founder

Meet Russell Fedell — Founder of PureVida

Russell Fedell is the founder of PureVida, a company built on the belief that what we consume every day should protect our health, our families, and our future.

With years of experience in business leadership and operations, Russell has dedicated his career to creating premium water and hydration solutions that are reliable, sustainable, and designed for real life — homes, families, and workplaces alike.

At the core of everything Russell builds is family. He is a devoted husband and a proud father of three boys, and that role shapes every decision he makes. As a parent, Russell understands that clean water isn't just a convenience — it's a responsibility. It's about protecting children, supporting healthy development, and creating a safe foundation for everyday living.

Through PureVida, Russell brings together innovation, integrity, and long-term thinking — delivering systems that improve health, reduce waste, and elevate the quality of life for families and businesses. His leadership blends practical execution with a deeply personal mission: to leave the world — and its water — better than he found it.
