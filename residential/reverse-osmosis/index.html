How It Works

Our advanced 5-stage filtration process ensures every drop of water is thoroughly purified.

Stage 1

Sediment/Carbon Pre-Filter

Removes larger particles like silt, rust, and scale while reducing chlorine to protect the membrane.

Stage 2 & 3

Reverse Osmosis Membrane

The heart of the system – a Thin Film Composite membrane that separates dissolved minerals from water.

Stage 4

Storage Tank

1.7-gallon pressurized tank stores purified water for instant access when you need it.

Stage 5

Carbon Post-Filter

Final polish removes any remaining tastes and odors for crystal-clear, great-tasting water.
