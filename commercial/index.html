A Message From the Founder

About the Founder — Russell Fedell

At PureVida, we believe water is more than a utility — it's infrastructure.

Businesses depend on consistency, reliability, and performance in every part of their operation. Yet water — one of the most essential inputs for employees, customers, equipment, and daily processes — is often overlooked. That gap is where PureVida was built.

I founded PureVida to raise the standard for commercial water, ice, and beverage systems. Our mission is simple: deliver premium, reliable hydration solutions that improve operational efficiency, employee experience, and long-term cost control — without compromising sustainability or quality.

We partner with organizations that care about performance and reputation — offices, healthcare facilities, fitness centers, schools, and manufacturing environments — providing systems that are engineered to scale, easy to maintain, and designed for real-world use.

PureVida is not about selling equipment. It's about delivering better water quality, lower waste, and a better experience for the people who rely on your business every day.

Thank you for considering PureVida as your trusted water partner. We look forward to helping your organization operate better — starting with the water that fuels it.
